Sinopsi and Information

    Peaky Blinders is a BBC drama that tells the story of the gypsy family Shelby and their gang of gangsters, a group of people who are known for their shiny berets and ownership of illegal businesses, who dominate the underground gambling industry and are rewarded through extortion.

    The story begins in Birmingham (the United Kingdom) in the 1920s when a young Tomas (Tommy) Shelby returns from the war after fighting against the French to take over the family business run, during his absence, by his aunt Polly Gray.

    However, once he arrives back home, there are problems. Running a criminal business is no easy task, especially if the psychological scars of being at war are still present. In addition, political disagreements create a rift between his best childhood friend Freddie, with communist ideas, and his sister Ada, who maintain a relationship far away from the family and, above all, from Tommy.

    The disputes also appear in the field of the professional, because he has several encounters with different enemies, the most important being a new police captain from Belfast who has proposed to clean the place of criminals, gangsters and anyone who is against the laws that the country dictates, his main task being to recover some weapons intercepted by Shelby.

    Peaky Blinders stars Cillian Murphy, star of Llums vermelles; and Sam Neill (Pla de Fuita). In addition, the creator and screenwriter of the series is none other than Steven Knight, creator of the story of Promises of the East.

Seasons

    Season 1: Thomas Shelby, leader of the Peaky Blinders gang, gets his hands on a shipment of guns from the local factory. Thomas wants to have these weapons in order to increase the power of his gang, but his aunt asks him to get rid of them. His dream, to have a legal business of horse racing, and to get into bed with the only man (Billy Kimber) who can help him to achieve it, creates many conflicts. On the other hand, a detective from Belfast arrives in Brimingham with an objective. This detective, called Chester Campbel, wants to put an end to the violent gangs that do whatever they want in the city.

    Second season: Set in the early 1920's. An alarming and dangerous event raises Inspector Campbell's suspicions about Tommy. Meanwhile, the gang leader moves forward with his business by expanding with the head of the London underworld (Alfie Solomons). This union, creates to Tommy several curses. This is due to the fact that he gets into dangerous territories that endanger the gang.

    Third season: Takes place in 1924, two years after the previous season. At the beginning of this season, Tommy marries Grace. For the people, Tommy is dedicated to horse racing and selling cars. However, the leader works in secret for Inspector Campbell. The Shelby family enters a very dangerous world on an international level. This jeopardizes the whole organization, so the leader begins to question his dreams and ambitions.

    Season 4: Each member of the Shelby family receives a mysterious letter on Christmas Eve and learns they are in danger. One year after the Shelby's estrangement, the family becomes the target of some gangsters. This forces them to unite once again, to agree on a plot between them and to look for another ally. When the mob closes in on Tommy, he leaves his home and returns to the streets of Birmingham where he begins a desperate struggle for survival.

    Fifth season: The story of the Peaky Blinders and the Shelby family is immersed in the chaos of the financial crisis of 1929. The protagonist, Tommy Shelby, is now a member of parliament and is approached by a charismatic politician to propose something that could affect his family and even the future of the nation.

    Sixth season: With Tommy Shelby on the verge of suicide, the sixth season is set at a key moment in the evolution of the history of the United Kingdom. A change that would bring about a transformation in many things at a political, social and economic level: the Battle of Cable Street, which would mean the end of feixism in the country.
