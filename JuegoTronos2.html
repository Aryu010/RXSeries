He is forced to leave his homeland to join the court of King Robert Baratheon 'The Usurper' (Mark Addy, 'Robin Hood', 'The Stonecroppers in Viva Rock Hortes'), lord of the Seven Kingdoms, a wayward man and once a great warrior. From his new position closer to the king, he will occupy the ideal place to try to unravel a tangle of intrigues that will jeopardize both his life and that of the members of his family: his wife Catelyn (Michelle Fairley, 'Harry Potter') and their children Robb (Richard Madden, 'Sirens'), Bran (Isaac Hempstead-*Wright), Arya (Maisie Williams), Sansa (Sophie Turner) and the bastard Jon Snow (Kit Harington).
