Sinopsi and Information

    On April 26th, 1986, one of the worst human catastrophes ever to hit the face of the earth. The Chernobyl nuclear power plant, which at that time belonged to the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, explodes causing one of the greatest environmental disasters in history, due to the overheating of the nuclear reactor's nuclear reactor.

    This series focuses on the men and women who sacrificed their lives to save the rest of Europe from even greater consequences than they had suffered. In addition, it revolves around the figure of Boris Shcherbina, Vice President of the Council of Ministers and head of the USSR Fuel and Energy Bureau, who is assigned the task of leading the Chernobyl government commission during the first hours of the accident, before all the facts and the serious consequences were known.

    Shcherbina is now caught up in a political and bureaucratic system that leaves much to be desired in the face of the human losses caused.

    Seasons
