Sinopsi and Information

    This new History Channel historical drama is centered on Ragnar Lothbrok, a mythical figure who claimed to be the descendant of Odin, the main god of Norse mythology.

    In it, Ragnar Lothbrok has a dream, which is to explore new civilizations by sailing the seas. Thanks to the help of his friend Floki, a buffoon, they build new, faster and more elegant ships with which to give shape to their greatest dream. In their adventure they meet Earl Haraldson, Gabriel Byrne, their main enemy, who is suspicious of Lothbrok's expectations. The fight between them is not long in coming.

    Lothbrok manages to become the best warrior of the Viking tribe as he struggles to become the king of Denmark and much of what is now Sweden. The series highlights the most imaginative battles, with more emphasis on the individual points of view, strategies and cunning of the characters.

Seasons

    The number of chapters per season of this series goes from 9 episodes to 20, so it is a story with a long and detailed plot.

    First season: it has 9 chapters among which "Ritu de passatge", the first of the whole series, stands out, as well as "El rescat d'un rei" and "Tot canvia", with which this first part will end.

    Second season: made up of 10 episodes, it premiered in February 2014 with "Guerra del germà" and had other outstanding episodes such as "Sense perdó".

    Third season: it was another 10-chapter series and debuted in February 2015. Here we will cover chapters such as "The usurper", "At the gates" i "The dead"

    Fourth season: this is the beginning of longer productions of 20 chapters divided in 2 installments. The first half of the season started with "The last boat", while the second half opened with "The stranger".

    Fifth season: it also has 20 chapters divided in two installments with episodes such as "the fisherman king", "Moments of vision" and "Ragnarok".

    Sixth season: The sixth season focuses on King Bjorn's rule over Kattigat and Ivar's adventures in Rus.
